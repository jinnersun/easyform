EasyForm

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

When someone submits a form through EasyForm, we process:

  • The form field values the visitor entered (name, email, message, etc.)
  • The notification email address you configured (so we know where to deliver submissions)
  • Submission timestamps (for rate limiting purposes)

2. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not store form submissions in a database
  • We do not use cookies or tracking scripts
  • We do not collect IP addresses or browser fingerprints
  • We do not run analytics on your visitors

3. How We Use Data

Form submissions are processed ephemerally on Cloudflare's edge network:

  • The submission is forwarded to your configured email address immediately
  • AI spam detection runs on the submission content at the edge
  • After email delivery, the submission is discarded — it is not retained

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any form data with third parties. Email delivery goes through Mailchannels (a Cloudflare-associated email relay). AI processing runs on Cloudflare Workers AI at the edge.

5. Data Security

All data transmission between the visitor's browser and EasyForm uses HTTPS. The Worker runs on Cloudflare's global edge network and does not persist form data to disk.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, contact us at the email below.
